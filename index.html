Our Story

A Kitchen Full of Memories

❈

Zara Kitchen is a family-run restaurant in the heart of London, born from a deep love of Middle Eastern culinary heritage. Every dish carries the warmth of a grandmother's kitchen — slow-cooked stews fragrant with cumin and coriander, smoky grilled meats marinated overnight, and desserts that taste of rose water and memory.

We draw our inspiration from the vibrant food cultures of Lebanon, Syria, Jordan, and Palestine, bringing their finest flavours to your table with honesty and care. Whether you're sharing a mezze with old friends or discovering these tastes for the first time, you are always welcome here. This is our home — and tonight, it is yours.
